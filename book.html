vii · inheritance
— you have inherited —
you did not
arrive
empty.
you have inherited
the small accumulated kindnesses
of everyone who was there before you.
you will leave something too —
not a name, not a story.
a quality the next person will quietly trust.

epilogue
— end of file —
soon you will close this book.
soon you will look up.

whatever you see —
the wall, the window,
a face, your hands —

you will be looking at the inside
of the same thing
you have always been the inside of.
go gently.
you have always been
bloom.
